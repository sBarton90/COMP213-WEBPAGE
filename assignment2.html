Protected Areas, and Their Importance

There are many reasons why we should have protected areas locally, nationally, and globally. We are the dominant species on this planet but that does not mean we do not have an obligation to protect and preserve the other species that we share this planet with. So much of our time each day is spent with our heads down while we work that we sometimes forget the importance of our impact on the planet. Now more than ever it is important that we work together to help support this effort of preservation.
